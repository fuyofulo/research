# Brex - Research Folder

Compiled May 2026 using the company research playbook.

## Headline finding

Brex is a mature financial operations platform for startups, mid-market companies, and enterprises. It began as a corporate card for startups, expanded into spend management, business accounts, bill pay, travel, accounting automation, APIs, and AI agents, and is now a Capital One-owned business after Capital One completed the acquisition on April 7, 2026.

The honest one-liner: Brex is the benchmark fiat-native finance OS for modern companies: cards + expenses + AP + travel + business banking + accounting automation, with AI agents layered on top and regulated functions handled through bank, broker-dealer, card, and money-transmission partners.

## Why it matters

Brex matters to our research because it shows what the non-stablecoin version of the "AI CFO stack" looks like at scale:

- The wedge was not AI. The original wedge was better startup credit underwriting and no-personal-guarantee corporate cards.
- The expansion path was workflow control: expenses, approvals, budgets, bill pay, accounting sync, travel, reimbursements, and APIs.
- The current AI story is credible because Brex controls a lot of structured spend, policy, vendor, card, payment, budget, and accounting data.
- The regulated substrate is partner-heavy: Brex is not a bank, but wraps bank accounts, card issuing, money transmission, treasury, and partner programs into one product.
- The Capital One acquisition proves banks want this software layer instead of only issuing cards and holding deposits.

For our stablecoin/AI CFO idea, Brex is the company to study if we want to understand the workflow depth we would eventually need beyond just on-ramp/off-ramp and wallets.
