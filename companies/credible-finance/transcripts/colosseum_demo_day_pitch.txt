Hi, I'm Shri, co-founder and CEO at Credible. Credible is the first payment gateway built on stablecoins.

Stablecoins solved the global money movement, but the payment gateways are still taking 3 to 7 days for settlement. Gateways like Stripe and Adyen — they process over $20 trillion worth of payments every year. And the minimum settlement times is 3 days, and can be up to 11 days.

That's where Credible comes in. Credible combines the traditional payment gateway infrastructure with stablecoin liquidity to offer T+0 settlement, better FX rate, and local payment method coverage.

We are already live in US-India corridor and we will be soon live in other markets. In last 5 months of our operations, we have already processed over $350 million worth of transactions.
