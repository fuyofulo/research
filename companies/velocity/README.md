# Velocity (velocity.xyz) - Research Folder

Compiled May 2026 using the company research playbook.

## Headline finding

Velocity is an enterprise stablecoin payments and treasury infrastructure company. The product is aimed at CFOs, treasurers, payment companies, global businesses, and financial institutions that need to move money across banks, blockchains, currencies, and jurisdictions without waiting on traditional banking cutoff windows.

The honest one-liner: Velocity is trying to become the stablecoin-native operating layer for enterprise treasury: API-first accounts/wallets, cross-border transfers, stablecoin settlement, FX/liquidity routing, compliance controls, institutional custody, and TMS/ERP integrations. It is much more serious than a thin wrapper because the team and partner stack are strong, but much of the product is still gated, partner-dependent, and not publicly testable.

## Why it matters to us

Velocity is a direct competitor to the "AI-native CFO stack on stablecoin rails" idea, but it is taking the high-end enterprise route:

- Enterprise-first, not SMB-first.
- Stablecoin infrastructure, not crypto app UX.
- API + treasury platform, not only dashboard.
- Banks/blockchains/borders positioning, not Solana-specific.
- Strong regulated-finance operators and institutional partners.

This leaves room for us to start smaller: Solana-native treasury workflows, operator-friendly finance automation, AI reconciliation, approval intelligence, and a tight wedge around specific customer workflows instead of trying to build a global banking network from day one.
