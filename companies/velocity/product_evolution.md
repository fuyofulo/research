# Velocity - Product Evolution

Date: 2026-05-07

## Timeline

### January 2025 - incorporation

Velocity Technology Holdings Limited was incorporated in the UK on 23 January 2025 as Velocity Lab Holdings Limited, then renamed Velocity Technology Holdings Limited on 29 January 2025.

Source: [Companies House](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/company/16204089).

### May 2025 - stealth launch and $10M pre-seed

Velocity emerged from stealth with a $10M pre-seed and announced the Stablecoin Payment Account. The round was led by Activant Capital with participation from Fuel Ventures, Triton Capital, Fabric Ventures, Commerce Ventures, Digital Space Ventures, and Preface Ventures. The company framed itself as infrastructure for enterprises to move and manage capital across banks, blockchains, and borders.

Source: [GlobeNewswire launch release](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2025/05/28/3089291/0/en/Velocity-comes-out-of-stealth-with-10M-to-power-the-Velocity-of-Money.html).

### October 2025 - Zodia Custody

Velocity announced Zodia Custody as custodian. The post emphasizes institutional custody, regulated environments, EMI permissions, MiCA, and global licensing work.

Source: [Zodia partnership](https://www.velocity.xyz/blog-post/velocity-zodiacustody).

### October 2025 - Dragonfly investment

Velocity announced Dragonfly Capital invested in the company. The post frames stablecoins as moving from edge use cases to core financial infrastructure and emphasizes custody, liquidity, regulatory alignment, and operational controls.

Source: [Dragonfly investment](https://www.velocity.xyz/blog-post/dragonfly-invests-in-velocity).

### November 2025 - Fireblocks strategic partnership

Velocity announced Fireblocks as a strategic infrastructure partner for secure wallet architecture, self-custody/governance controls, approval flows, policy enforcement, segregation of assets, and transaction infrastructure.

Source: [Fireblocks partnership](https://www.velocity.xyz/blog-post/velocity-x-fireblocks-strategic-partnership).

### January-March 2026 - thought leadership and market education

Velocity published stablecoin treasury thought pieces, including 2026 stablecoin predictions, emerging markets treasury, liquidity, and a founder letter. The March 2026 founder letter says Velocity is live.

Source: [blog](https://www.velocity.xyz/blog), [Welcome to Velocity](https://www.velocity.xyz/blog-post/welcome-to-velocity).

### 2026 current positioning

The current homepage positions Velocity as "a next-gen enterprise platform for payments and treasury solutions. Powered by stablecoins. Built for CFOs." It emphasizes payments, settlement, treasury, TMS/ERP integrations, programmable regulated wallets, yield optimization, enterprise security, and one API.

Source: [homepage](https://www.velocity.xyz/).

## Strategic shift noticed

The May 2025 launch release named Tom Greenwood and Eric Queathem as founders, with Greenwood quoted as co-founder and CEO and Queathem as co-founder and President. Current 2026 public posts frame Eric Queathem as Founder and CEO, and the current About page does not list Greenwood in the leadership team.

This is important but should not be over-interpreted without direct confirmation. Companies House still lists Greenwood as an active PSC, while current public leadership centers Eric and a new operating team.
