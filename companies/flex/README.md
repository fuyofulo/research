# Flex (flex.one) - Research Folder

Compiled May 2026 using the company research playbook.

## Headline finding

Flex is an AI-native finance platform for business owners, not a stablecoin company. It started as Flexbase, a construction-focused credit/card product, then broadened into a business-owner finance platform: business banking, Net-60 credit card, employee expense management, global payments, AP automation, AR automation, and an emerging "AI CFO"/agentic ERP layer.

The honest one-liner: Flex is trying to become the private bank + Brex + AP/expense stack for middle-market owner-operators, using partner banks/card issuers for regulated rails and AI agents/workflows to automate the back office.

## Why it matters

Flex is relevant to our research because it attacks the same "CFO/back-office operating layer" from the fiat side:

- Owner-first positioning, not bookkeeper-first.
- Business + personal finance convergence as the long-term wedge.
- Credit/float as primary acquisition hook.
- Bill Pay/AP automation as workflow expansion.
- Global payments as a payments wedge.
- AI agents as the product narrative for underwriting, AP/AR, expenses, cash optimization, and owner intelligence.

This is the fiat-native version of the product category we are thinking about. Stablecoin competitors sell new rails. Flex sells better owner finance on existing bank/card rails.
