# Flex - Product Evolution

Date: 2026-05-09

## 2021 - Flexbase construction wedge

Flexbase began with construction-focused payments/credit. TechCrunch described it as launching a construction-industry credit card with up to 60 days interest-free financing, founded by Zaid Rahman and Hadi Solh.

Source: [TechCrunch 2021](https://techcrunch.com/2021/11/11/flexbase-unveils-construction-industrys-first-credit-card/).

## 2022 - first Flex card swipe

The company timeline says 2022 included the first Flex card swipe and first five employees.

Source: [company page](https://www.flex.one/company).

## 2023 - credit card, Ghost Financial, $120M debt/equity

The company says it launched Flex Credit Card, hit $1M in transactions, acquired Ghost Financial, and raised $120M in debt and equity. PRNewswire/Covington describe the 2023 financing as $20M Series A equity led by Florida Funders plus $100M debt facility from CIM Group.

Sources: [company page](https://www.flex.one/company), [PRNewswire 2023](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/flex-raises-120-million-in-equity-and-debt-launching-finance-super-app-for-business-owners-301931368.html), [Covington](https://www.cov.com/news-and-insights/news/2023/09/covington-advises-flexbase-technologies-on-120-million-equity-and-debt-financings).

## 2024 - banking, global payments, B2B payments

The company timeline says Flex launched Banking, Global Payments, and B2B Payments in 2024. This is the key transition from card-led product to broader finance suite.

Source: [company page](https://www.flex.one/company).

## March 2025 - $25M equity + $200M credit facility

TechCrunch reported Flex raised $25M in equity at just under $250M valuation and secured a $200M credit facility from Victory Park Capital. The article says Flex marketed itself as an all-in-one finance platform for mid-market business owners to manage finances from revenue to personal spend. It also mentions AI underwriting, AI invoice processing, and expense management.

Source: [TechCrunch 2025](https://techcrunch.com/2025/03/05/flex-a-brex-for-business-owners-has-raised-25m-at-a-250m-valuation/).

## 2025 - $1B transactions and Bill Pay / AP automation

Company timeline says Flex hit $1B in transactions and launched Bill Pay/AP automation in 2025.

Source: [company page](https://www.flex.one/company).

## Late 2025 / current - $60M Series B and AI-native private bank narrative

Flex announced a $60M Series B led by Portage and says total equity raised is $105M. The announcement frames Flex as an AI-native private bank for high-net-worth middle-market business owners, with AI agents across private credit, business finance, personal finance, payments, and ERP. It says Flex grew revenue 4x, launched personal finance, tripled private credit offerings, and introduced AI-powered tools.

Source: [Series B announcement](https://www.flex.one/resource-hub/flex-raises-60m-series-b).

## Current product shape

Flex is now a layered finance suite:

1. Entry wedge: Net-60 card / credit.
2. Retention layer: business banking and AP payments.
3. Workflow layer: expenses, bill pay, approvals, accounting sync.
4. Expansion layer: global payments, pay-by-card, AR.
5. Narrative/future layer: personal finance, private banking, Owner Intelligence, AI CFO, agentic ERP.

## Evolution pattern

Flex evolved from construction cash-flow financing into a general owner-finance platform. The strategic constant is cash-flow pain; the target customer broadened from contractors to middle-market business owners.
