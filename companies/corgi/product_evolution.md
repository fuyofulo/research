# Corgi - Product Evolution

Date: 2026-05-08

## 2024 - founding and YC

Corgi was founded in 2024 by Nico Laqua and Emily Yuan. YC lists it as Summer 2024. The original YC positioning: "Corgi is building an AI financial infrastructure company." The launch post already framed Corgi as a full-stack insurance carrier for startups, not a broker.

Source: [YC profile](https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/corgi-insurance).

## July 2025 - regulatory approval

Corgi's January 2026 funding release says the company received regulatory approval in July 2025 to launch as a licensed carrier. The Series B release repeats that since receiving regulatory approval in July 2025, Corgi launched the first AI-native, full-stack insurance platform purpose-built for startups.

Sources: [PRNewswire Jan 2026](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/corgi-insurance-raises-108-million-receives-regulatory-approval-to-launch-the-first-full-stack-insurance-carrier-for-startups-302657727.html), [Series B release](https://www.corgi.insure/press-releases/series-b).

## 2025 - culture and brand visibility

Corgi drew attention in 2025 for intense AI startup work culture and viral office/culture stories. This created unusual brand awareness for an insurance startup. The site references Forbes, Economic Times, Wall Street Journal, Inc., and Insurance Business coverage.

Source: [homepage](https://www.corgi.insure/).

## Late 2025 / early 2026 - ETF expansion

Corgi Strategies launched the Founder-Led ETF (FDRS), listed on Nasdaq in late Dec 2025 / early Jan 2026. Corgi then launched Founder-Led 2x Daily ETF (FDRX). SEC filings show Corgi Strategies, LLC as adviser to Corgi ETF Trust I.

Sources: [PRNewswire FDRS release](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/corgi-an-ai-startup-lists-fdrs-on-nasdaq-302651983.html), [SEC filing](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/2078265/000207826526000003/corgietftrust1_fdrsfdrx497.htm).

## January 2026 - $108M seed + Series A

Corgi announced $108M across seed and Series A. The capital was for scaling startup insurance, expanding coverage and distribution, and investing in AI systems for underwriting, claims, and policy operations. The release claimed ARR surpassed $40M since full regulatory approval in July 2025.

Source: [PRNewswire Jan 2026](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/corgi-insurance-raises-108-million-receives-regulatory-approval-to-launch-the-first-full-stack-insurance-carrier-for-startups-302657727.html).

## May 2026 - $160M Series B / unicorn

Corgi announced a $160M Series B at a $1.3B valuation, led by TCV. Total funding rose to over $268M. The release says the new capital supports broader coverage, deeper distribution, and AI systems across underwriting, claims, and policy operations. It also says Corgi is moving into trucking as a new vertical.

Source: [Series B release](https://www.corgi.insure/press-releases/series-b).

## Current product shape

Today, Corgi has three related product directions:

1. Startup insurance: core wedge and customer-facing product.
2. Broader insurance vertical expansion: trucking, payroll, small business mentioned in Series B leadership quote.
3. Financial products/ETFs: Founder-Led ETFs through Corgi Strategies.

## Evolution pattern

Corgi is evolving from "startup insurance checkout" into "AI financial infrastructure." The public product that makes money and proves demand is insurance. The broader ambition is a financial product platform that can design, distribute, and operate regulated financial products faster than incumbents.
