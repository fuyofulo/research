## Reliability notes

- Best truth sources: Disclaimers, producer licenses, SEC filings.
- Best product sources: homepage and customer pages.
- Best company/funding sources: YC profile and press releases, cross-checked by TechCrunch.
- Weakest claims: "first", "$40M ARR", "cheaper", "AI-native" depth, and customer outcomes that are not customer-authored outside Corgi.
