## Biggest tension

The biggest tension is between the simple founder-facing promise and the actual insurance stack.

Founder-facing promise:

"Get insured fast. We own the carrier. No broker chaos."

Legal/operational reality:

"Coverage may be underwritten by affiliated or third-party carriers, distributed by licensed producer entities, administered by affiliated or third-party claims operations, potentially reinsured, and subject to jurisdiction/product availability."

This is normal in insurance, but it is exactly where diligence must focus.
