# KAST - Use Cases and Examples

Date: 2026-05-08

## Use case 1: Crypto worker spends stablecoins like money

User:

A crypto worker or trader receives USDC/USDT on Solana, Ethereum, Arbitrum, or Tron and wants to spend without manually off-ramping through an exchange.

Flow:

1. User deposits supported stablecoin into KAST.
2. KAST credits USD balance.
3. User adds virtual card to Apple Pay/Google Pay.
4. User pays at a merchant.
5. KAST handles conversion/settlement behind the scenes.

Why KAST wins:

- Stablecoins become card-spendable immediately.
- No separate exchange/off-ramp step.
- Visa acceptance solves merchant coverage.

Public proof:

Homepage includes user testimonials about topping up with SOL/USDT, paying Deliveroo via Apple Pay in minutes, using KAST in Argentina, and replacing traditional cards while traveling.

Source: [homepage testimonials](https://www.kast.xyz/).

## Use case 2: Freelancer gets paid in USD without U.S. residency

User:

A freelancer outside the U.S. works with American clients or platforms and wants USD account details.

Flow:

1. User completes KYC.
2. KAST creates a virtual USD account.
3. Freelancer gives ACH/Fedwire details to client/platform.
4. Payment arrives into KAST Cash.
5. User spends via card, withdraws stablecoin, or sends local payout.

Why KAST wins:

- User gets U.S.-style account details without U.S. residency.
- USD balance protects against local currency volatility.
- Card and payouts make funds usable.

Sources: [Global Accounts](https://www.kast.xyz/global-accounts), [USD account support](https://concierge.kast.xyz/hc/en-us/articles/13971393267087-How-Do-I-Create-A-USD-Account-on-KAST), [freelancer page](https://www.kast.xyz/freelancers).

## Use case 3: High-inflation market user stores value in digital dollars

User:

A user in Argentina, Nigeria, Turkey, or another market with weak local currency wants dollar exposure and global spending.

Flow:

1. User receives local currency or crypto.
2. User converts into stablecoin/USD balance.
3. User keeps value in KAST.
4. User spends locally via Visa or sends local payouts.

Why KAST wins:

- USD/stablecoin account can be more useful than local bank balance.
- Card acceptance bridges the real world.
- Local payout rails reduce withdrawal friction.

Public proof:

KAST has country-specific crypto card pages and user testimonials for Argentina, plus payout rails including ARS, BRL/PIX, MXN/SPEI, NGN, INR, PHP, THB, and others.

Sources: [fees page](https://concierge.kast.xyz/hc/en-us/articles/9850062738703-What-Are-the-Fees-and-Conditions-for-KAST-Cards-and-Accounts), [Argentina crypto card page](https://www.kast.xyz/crypto-cards/argentina).

## Use case 4: Global consumer sends local payout

User:

Someone wants to send money to family, a vendor, or themselves in another country.

Flow:

1. Fund KAST with stablecoin, crypto, or bank transfer.
2. Choose recipient country and currency.
3. KAST displays FX/fees.
4. Licensed PSP executes payout.
5. Recipient receives local currency.

Why KAST wins:

- Single app abstracts stablecoin-to-local-fiat movement.
- Fees are visible before transfer.
- Stablecoin rails can make funding faster.

Sources: [Global Payouts](https://www.kast.xyz/global-payouts), [fees page](https://concierge.kast.xyz/hc/en-us/articles/9850062738703-What-Are-the-Fees-and-Conditions-for-KAST-Cards-and-Accounts).

## Use case 6: Business payouts and team cards

User:

A global agency/startup pays contractors, creators, media buyers, and vendors in multiple countries.

Flow:

1. Business joins KAST Business waitlist.
2. Business holds dollars, USDC, and SOL in one view.
3. Business creates virtual cards per vendor/person.
4. Business sends payouts to teams/vendors in 170+ countries.
5. Business gets cashback and cloud-service benefits.

Why KAST could win:

- Global contractor payouts are painful.
- Cards with controls are a real SMB/agency workflow.
- Crypto-first teams already hold stablecoins.

Important caveat:

KAST Business appears early/waitlisted. Public page says applications are reviewed by hand and next wave opens early May.

Source: [KAST Business](https://www.kast.xyz/business).

## Pattern across use cases

KAST wins by turning stablecoins into daily financial utility:

- Stablecoins in.
- USD ledger balance.
- Visa spend or local payout out.
- Rewards and memberships on top.
- Business controls later.

The product is strongest when users already have a reason to hold or receive digital dollars.
