## Main unresolved tension

KAST is extremely good at making stablecoins feel like consumer banking. But consumer banking expectations include deposit protection, clear legal claim on funds, predictable limits, and regulated recourse. KAST's own legal terms make clear this is a partner-dependent virtual asset service with non-bank risk.

That tension is the company.
