# KAST (kast.xyz) - Research Folder

Compiled May 2026 using the company research playbook.

## Headline finding

KAST is a stablecoin-powered neobank/card app for global consumers and businesses. It gives users a USD account, stablecoin deposits/withdrawals, local payouts, Visa cards, rewards/memberships, and yield products. The company is positioning itself as "Revolut rebuilt from scratch on Web3": a borderless money app where users can receive USD via ACH/Fedwire, deposit USDC/USDT/USDe/PYUSD/RLUSD across multiple chains, spend through Visa, send local payouts, and earn through vaults.

The honest one-liner: KAST is a fast-growing custodial stablecoin neobank that uses licensed partners for cards, banking, custody, ramps, and local payouts, while building its own brand, wallet/ledger UX, membership economics, rewards, and proprietary M0-powered stablecoin layer.

## Why it matters

KAST is one of the clearest consumer-facing examples of stablecoin financial services going mainstream:

- 1M+ users claimed.
- Nearly $5B annualized transaction volume claimed.
- $80M Series A in March 2026.
- $600M valuation reported by Bloomberg/Blockhead.
- USD accounts via partner bank.
- Visa cards across 150M merchants.
- Multichain stablecoin deposits.
- Local payouts via PIX, SPEI, ACH, SEPA, SWIFT, and other rails.
- Business product moving toward payroll, team cards, and global vendor payouts.

For our AI/stablecoin CFO-stack thinking, KAST is less like Velocity and more like the consumer/global-worker version of the same thesis: stablecoins as the new account substrate, partner banks at the fiat edge, cards for spend, and a product layer that hides crypto complexity.
