# KAST - Product Evolution

Date: 2026-05-08

## 2024 - launch and seed

KAST launched around July 2024 according to company/job copy and Series A materials. In December 2024, KAST announced a $10M seed led by HSG/HongShan and Peak XV Partners, with angels, DST Global partners, and Goodwater involvement. The original pitch was a stablecoin-based neobank with payment cards and USD-denominated accounts in 150+ countries.

Sources: [seed blog](https://www.kast.xyz/blog/kast-secures-us-10-million-seed-round), [Chainwire repost](https://www.chainbits.com/press-releases/kast-secures-us10-million-seed-round-led-by-hsg-hongshan-capital-group-and-peak-xv-partners/).

## Early 2025 - stablecoin banking/category formation

KAST expanded the core product around crypto cards, stablecoin banking country pages, and USD account/card workflows. Public copy emphasized "spend stablecoins anywhere Visa is accepted," global reach, and crypto-to-card usability.

Source: [homepage](https://www.kast.xyz/).

## April 2025 - M0/Solana partnership

M0 announced expansion to Solana and named KAST as the first company to leverage M0's stablecoin platform on Solana. KAST planned two digital dollars: one for tokenizing deposits/payments and one for savings/yield. KAST described this as a way to represent customer deposits on-chain and create transparent branded digital dollars.

Sources: [M0 announcement](https://www.m0.org/press-releases/m-0-launches-on-solana-bringing-the-first-programmable-stablecoin-infrastructure-to-one-of-cryptos-fastest-growing-ecosystem), [KAST stablecoin blog](https://www.kast.xyz/blog/kast-strengthens-its-banking-alternative-platform-with-its-own-stablecoins).

## Late 2025 - virtual USD accounts

KAST announced virtual accounts, allowing users to send and receive funds through Fedwire and ACH. KAST framed this as eliminating the need to convert fiat to crypto through exchanges before using the app.

Source: [virtual accounts blog](https://www.kast.xyz/blog/virtual-accounts-are-now-available-in-kast).

## Early 2026 - help-center maturity and product expansion

By early 2026, KAST support docs had detailed pages for:

- USD account creation.
- Supported stablecoins/networks.
- Fees and limits.
- Country restrictions.
- Card availability.

This signals a real operating product, not just marketing.

Sources: [USD account support](https://concierge.kast.xyz/hc/en-us/articles/13971393267087-How-Do-I-Create-A-USD-Account-on-KAST), [fees](https://concierge.kast.xyz/hc/en-us/articles/9850062738703-What-Are-the-Fees-and-Conditions-for-KAST-Cards-and-Accounts), [supported stablecoins](https://concierge.kast.xyz/hc/en-us/articles/11784729022863-Which-Stablecoins-Are-Supported-on-the-KAST-App).

## March 2026 - $80M Series A

KAST announced an $80M Series A co-led by QED Investors and Left Lane Capital, with Peak XV, HSG, and DST Global Partners participating. KAST said it had more than 1M users, close to $5B annualized transaction volume, and revenue doubled since September 2025. Funds were allocated to North America, Latin America, Middle East expansion, licensing, compliance, product, and headcount.

Sources: [Series A blog](https://www.kast.xyz/blog/kast-announces-80m-series-a), [PRNewswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/kast-raises-80-million-as-stablecoins-move-from-infrastructure-into-mainstream-financial-services-302708234.html).

## 2026 - KAST Earn / USDKY

KAST Earn currently shows two vaults:

- USD Prime Vault with USDKY on Solana, powered by M0 and backed by short-term U.S. Treasuries.
- Gauntlet Alpha Vault on Base with DeFi stablecoin strategies.

This moves KAST from spend/account utility into savings/yield.

Source: [KAST Earn](https://www.kast.xyz/earn).

## 2026 - KAST Business

KAST Business is moving into global accounts, instant payouts, team cards, payroll, cross-border spending, and finance integrations. Public page suggests waitlist/hand-reviewed access with broader launch in 2026.

Source: [KAST Business](https://www.kast.xyz/business).

## Copy drift to watch

- Countries: 150+, 160+, 170+, 190+, 200+ appear across pages/press/support contexts.
- APY: homepage says up to 10%, Earn page says up to 7%.
- Cashback/rewards: older snippets mention 6%; current homepage/fees show 1.5%-3% USD cashback plus points.
- Business: some snippets say launching 2026; current page suggests early May access waves.
